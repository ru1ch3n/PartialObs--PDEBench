TL;DR

PRISMA turns PDE residuals into a frequency-aware attention signal inside a diffusion neural operator, aiming to preserve accuracy while cutting sampling steps.

Main contribution

  • Uses PDE residuals as *spectral attention* during diffusion sampling (guidance without extra solves).
  • Targets the speed–accuracy tradeoff in diffusion-based PDE inference under sparse measurements.
  • Shows improved quality vs prior diffusion guidance at low step counts on several PDE tasks.
